About Me

My name is Cory and I was born in Mesa, AZ but spent the majority of my life in Utah. My wife's name is Tana and we have two dogs and five chickens. We bought a house in Herriman after we lived in Frankfurt, Germany for 6 months. We had the opportunity to travel and see a lot of Europe and a few other countries. My favorite country was Iceland. Even though we were there in February and it was freezing and windy, it still offered the most beautiful sights I've ever seen, including the northern lights.
